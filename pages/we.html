the word “we.” has the power to tremendously reframe the way we see the world; it reminds us that we are not separate from anything else or anyone else.

no one and nothing is truly a closed system.

you would not be where you are or who you are without the context which you could not exist without.

further, in work contexts, using the word “we.” to describe work that was mostly your effort can make others feel valued and empower them to contribute, encouraging their ideation. it helps cultivate a community of people working together to realize a collective vision.
