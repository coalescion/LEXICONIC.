lexiconic. is a new project with a big vision. many significant updates are coming in the next year, and i will be working on the project full time beginning in october 2025.

to name a few priorities: 

some questions feel fundamental to the human condition. these include:
        * language compatibility
                - at lexiconic.global, we recognize that "global" is in the name. it is a priority that our platform is accesible to people
                  around the globe with a variety of mother tongues, and to do that we understand the need for pages or translations in a 
                  variety of languages. compatibility for spanish, french, german, and chinese are coming soon.
        * integration with artists
                - at its core,  lexiconic. is an artistic project, and aims to become a leading platform for artists to share their art.
                 many new features to promote artistic involvement are coming soon.
        * animation and apparel
                - we also have interest in making phrases more interactive via 2D animation, as well as create embroidered apparel to
                  support the project as a whole. apparel will include both general "lexiconic." designs, as well as designs based on 
                  specific phrases (yours included).

we are excited to bring these updates to the community, and are doing our best to make them happen. development will accelerate in october 2025.
