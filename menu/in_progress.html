lexiconic. is a new project with a big vision. many significant updates are coming in the next year, and we soon plan to work on the project full time.

to name a few priorities:

  • language compatibility
    • at lexiconic.global, we recognize that "global" is in the name. it is a priority that our platform is accesible to people around the globe with a variety of mother tongues, and to do that we understand the need for pages or translations in a variety of languages. compatibility for spanish, german, french, and chinese are coming soon.
  • integration with artists
    • at its core, lexiconic. is an artistic project, and aims to become a leading platform for artists to share their art. many new features to promote artistic involvement are coming soon.
  • animation and apparel
    • we also have interest in making phrases more interactive via 2D animation, as well as create embroidered apparel to support the project as a whole. apparel will include both general "lexiconic." designs, as well as designs based from specific phrases (yours included).

we are excited to bring these updates to the community, and are doing our best to make them happen. development will accelerate in 2026.
